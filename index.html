Proof-Of-Hack

University of Texas at Dallas | September 16-17, 2017

  • First-Ever Blockchain Hackathon at UTDallas.

    Proof-of-Hack HackDFW is 24 hours for you to learn, create, and make. For the whole weekend, students will make innovative projects focused on blockchain and cryptographic technology with help from cryptocurrency sponsors.

  • Where is Proof-of-Hack?

    Proof-of-Hack will be held at the UTDallas Makerspace Building at the University of Texas at Dallas. With both the first and second floor, anticipate a large open space for community hacking and the quiet floor for sleep and concentration. The address is 3000 Waterview Pkway, Richardson, TX 75080

  • Who is Proof-of-Hack for?

    Are you currently enrolled in a university or high school? Are you interested in building something awesome? Congratulations! You are eligible!

    HackDFW organizers want you to have fun, learn and explore. We look forward to making an open environment for all participants to explore their desire to learn more about the blockchain.
